Empowering Communities, One Step at a Time

Every project is a promise — to uplift, educate, and empower. Here's how Liz is making waves in grassroots change.

Wema Girls Cup

Empowering over 600 girls across 16 villages through soccer, life skills, and mentorship — reducing early pregnancies, early marriages, childhood sex and school dropouts.

Wema Technical Training Institute

Training women and youth in skills like baking, ICT, tailoring, masonry, accounting, and entrepreneurship to unlock self-reliance and opportunity.

Wema Water Project

Providing clean, accessible water to underserved communities, radically transforming health, sanitation, and dignity.

Wema BDO

Driving holistic community transformation through inclusive programs in education, health, empowerment, and sustainability.

I am very grateful to West TV for their excellent coverage of our inauguration event on 5th September that saw the inauguration of a farmers training school that will act as a training facility for farmers on agroforestry and it will also provide food to the 600 children at Wema Children Centre, in the presence of a 10 member delegation from the USA and India. This is the first school of forest farmers in Africa and I’m extremely delighted to continue to spearhead Wema’s efforts in food security and climate action.
